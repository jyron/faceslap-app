FaceSlap — Support

Thanks for playing FaceSlap! Here's how it works and how to get help.

How to play

Pick a face — snap a photo, choose one from your library, or tap “Slap a Random Face.” Then tap the face to slap it. Switch hands (Palm, Backhand, Flaming), watch the damage and combos build, hit Replay for slow-mo, and Share to export a clip.

Tips

  • Use a clear, front-facing photo for the best face detection.
  • Tap “Heal” to reset the face and start fresh.
  • Everything runs on your device — no internet required.

Privacy

FaceSlap collects no data; everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.
