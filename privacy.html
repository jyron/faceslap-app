FaceSlap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

FaceSlap is designed to respect your privacy completely.

What we collect

Nothing. FaceSlap does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Photos and camera

When you choose a photo or take one with the camera, the image is used only on your device to detect a face and run the game. Your photos and any videos you create never leave your device, are never uploaded to any server, and are not accessible to the developer or any third party. Face detection is performed entirely on-device using Apple's Vision framework.

Sharing

If you export a clip and choose to share it, that uses Apple's standard share sheet. What you do with the file afterward is governed by those apps' and services' own privacy policies, not FaceSlap.

Children

FaceSlap does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.
